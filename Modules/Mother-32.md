# Concept

The [[Mother-32]] is a distinctive analog instrument ([[Eurorack module]], [[Voltage controlled oscillator (VCO)]], [[Sequencer]]) that brings raw sound, step-sequencing and extensive interconnectivity to any electronic or modular ecosystem. This expansive live performance and production synthesizer is designed to provide experimental musicians and synthesists with a diverse array of creative tools to inspire new ideas, unique sound and endless sonic exploration.

# Concept

The [[Quad VCA]] is a compact and feature packed module ([[Eurorack module]]) that is sure to find its way it almost all of your patches. In addition to being used for standard VCA ([[Voltage controlled attenuator (VCA)]]) duties it can also be used as a mixer and signal booster.

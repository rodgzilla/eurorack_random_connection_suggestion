# Concept

[[Subharmonicon]] a semi-modular polyrhythmic ([[Polyrhythm]]) analog synthesizer ([[Eurorack module]]) that employs a 6-tone sound engine and multi-layered clock generator to explore the world of [[Subharmonic]]s, [[Polyrhythm]]s, and the unique relationships they create.
